WHO IS CHADWICK BOSEMAN

Chadwick was a famous actor , he is famous for his role as the black panther

HIS CAREER

He began his career with guest appearances on television and was a
series regular on the short-lived Lincoln Heights (2008–2009) and
Persons Unknown (2010). Boseman's breakthrough role came as baseball
player Jackie Robinson in the biopic 42 (2013).

WHAT HAPPENED

US actor Chadwick Boseman, best known for playing Black Panther in
the hit Marvel superhero franchise, has died of cancer aged 43. He
died at home in Los Angeles with his wife and family by his side, a
statement posted on social media said.
